About Me

As a night assistant manager at a Holiday Inn, my experience has been both challenging and rewarding. Working in a managerial role in the hospitality industry requires a lot of attention to detail and the ability to manage a team while also ensuring the comfort and satisfaction of our guests. I am responsible for overseeing all aspects of the hotel's operations during the night. This requires me to be highly organized and able to quickly respond to any issues that may arise. Another challenge is ensuring the safety and security of the hotel and our guests. This involves monitoring the building and ensuring that all emergency procedures are in place and working properly. It can be stressful to handle unexpected situations, such as power outages or guest complaints, but I have found that staying calm and solution focused helps to manage these situations effectively. In spite the challenges, I find the work to be incredibly rewarding. I enjoy working with the team to create a positive work environment and giving the staff a voice in decisions this helps keep staff included valued and insures staff retention within the department this allows our guests have a comfortable and enjoyable stay. I also appreciate the opportunity to develop my leadership skills and take on more responsibility within the hotel. Overall, working as a night assistant manager in a hotel requires a lot of hard work and dedication, but it has been a fulfilling and exciting career path.
